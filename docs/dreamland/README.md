## What Dreamland Is

Speciate today is a **portfolio showcase**: a high-performance artificial-life engine and visual sandbox demonstrating Rust x JS systems craft. The engineering work is real, validated, and measured.

**Dreamland is the dream on top of the engine.** It is the answer to "if this proves out, what could it *become*?" - a complete game with emotional stakes, taming, intelligent creatures, and eventually a shared multiplayer world.

Treat it as a vision board, not a plan of record. It is honest about being aspirational so that the rest of the documentation can stay honest about being verified.

---

## The Vision (North Star, Not a Schedule)

- **Steam Early Access** - a single-player desktop game (Electron) built on the proven engine. Pay-once, no servers. See [`project-spec.md`](./project-spec.md) and [`strategy/biz-strategy.md`](./strategy/biz-strategy.md).
- **The daughter-rescue narrative** - a thin narrative layer over a thick systemic ecosystem. You crash-land on an alien planet and must reach your daughter's pod across a living, evolving world. The simulation *is* the challenge. See [`narrative/goal.md`](./narrative/goal.md).
- **Taming** - beacon zones, harpoon capture, DNA cloning. Build a population, a shield, a scout network, an army. (Taming and related mechanics also live as concrete ideas in `docs/gameplay/ideas/`.)
- **Drongos** - intelligent, social creatures that become your companions and the emotional core of the world.
- **The Showdown** - the endgame gauntlet through apex (Karg) territory, solved through ecosystem mastery rather than a scripted boss fight. See [`narrative/showdown.md`](./narrative/showdown.md).
- **Phase 2 MMO** - the far-future expansion: a shared, persistent, browser-based world where players embody the evolving Drongo race.

## How To Read These Documents

- **Aspirational by default.** Dates, revenue figures, server-cost models, and phase timelines here are illustrative of the *vision*, not commitments. Some predate the portfolio-showcase repositioning and the zero-copy NAPI IPC architecture - read them as intent, not as current technical truth.
- **For current technical truth**, always defer to the code under `apps/`, the root `README.md`, and `docs/architecture/`.
- **The feature backlog is elsewhere.** Concrete, build-ready feature ideas (including taming and Drongo-flavored mechanics) live in `docs/biology/ideas/` and `docs/gameplay/ideas/`. Dreamland is the *why it all matters* layer above those.
