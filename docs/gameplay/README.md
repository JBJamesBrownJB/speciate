**Purpose:** Player-facing systems, controls, and loops that turn the artificial-life sandbox into something to *play with* — taming, territory, trophy hunting, scale-bar tools, world interactions, and the gameplay UI layer rendered in `apps/portal/`.

**This is a feature-lifecycle area.** Documents move through `ideas/` → (`todo/`) → `done/` as they are committed and built. The subfolders encode where each feature sits in that lifecycle:

| Subfolder | Category | Meaning |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| [`ideas/`](ideas/) | 💡 IDEAS | Brainstormed, **not committed**. Large backlog (~22 files: taming, territory control, trophy hunter, DNA collector, fishing, terrarium, etc.) plus the `interactions/` subfolder. No promise these get built. |
| [`done/`](done/) | ✅ DONE | **Implemented & working.** Currently just `minimap.md`. |

There is no `todo/` (📋 PLANNED) subfolder here yet — gameplay is mostly raw idea backlog. Add one when an idea is approved but not started.

**What's in progress NOW?** Not gameplay directly. Gameplay (Pillar 3 — Play) and biology are largely **backlog (💡) + already-shipped (✅)**. The active NOW pillars live in [`docs/scale/`](../scale/) (Prove Scale) and [`docs/visuals/`](../visuals/) (Prove Spectacle).
